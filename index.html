SAFIS - Somaliland Armed Forces Identification System

SAFIS is more than software — it’s a digital gateway to security and structure. Built for the Somaliland Armed Forces, this identification system brings accuracy and integrity to military personnel records. Every line of code is designed to serve the guardians of peace with dignity.

With its minimalist design and powerful backend, SAFIS ensures that every member of the armed forces is properly identified, recorded, and recognized. It empowers institutions to act with confidence and preserve the honor of those who serve.

This project is a testament to the power of vision, patriotism, and modern technology. Through SAFIS, we celebrate the brave, honor the organized, and bring innovation to national defense.
